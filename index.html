North American Crime Data

The app will be used to search Health and crime related information with in United States. Users will be able to interact with information using our search forms which can be submitted, and this will result in a response from the API with information related to health or Crime and a pin will be pointed on the Map after selecting your area. Two tabs will pop out with your pinpoint with health and crime information on each side, and you will be able to select which information you wish to view.
